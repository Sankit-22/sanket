Young & Budding Athletes

Why Anti-Doping Education for Young Athletes?

Young athletes are the future of Indian sports. It's crucial to educate them about the dangers of doping and the importance of clean sport.

Our Commitment

NADA India is committed to providing anti-doping education to young athletes identified at SAI's Khelo India Academies. We aim to instill the values of fair play and clean sport.

Education Programs

  • Interactive workshops and seminars
  • Educational videos and presentations
  • One-on-one counseling sessions
