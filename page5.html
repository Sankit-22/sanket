Why Anti-Doping Education for International Level Athletes?

International level athletes are role models for millions. It's crucial to ensure they uphold the highest standards of sportsmanship and fair play.

Our Commitment

NADA India is committed to providing comprehensive anti-doping education to TOPS athletes with any impairments participating in national and international events. We aim to create a level playing field for all athletes.

Education Programs

  • Customized workshops and seminars
  • Regular awareness campaigns
  • Online learning modules
  • One-on-one counseling sessions
