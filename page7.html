Athlete Support Personnel

Why Anti-Doping Education for Support Personnel?

Athlete support personnel play a crucial role in shaping an athlete's career. It's essential to educate them about anti-doping rules and regulations to ensure a clean sporting environment.

Our Commitment

NADA India is committed to providing anti-doping education to coaches, managers, trainers, agents, team staff, parents, sports medicine practitioners, physiotherapists, medical/para-medical personnel, and anti-doping practitioners. We aim to create a knowledgeable and informed support system for athletes.

Education Programs

  • Tailored workshops and seminars
  • Online learning modules
  • Resource materials and guidelines
  • One-on-one counseling sessions
