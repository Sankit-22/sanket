Registered Testing Pool (RTP) Athletes

Why Anti-Doping Education for RTP Athletes?

RTP athletes are subject to regular drug testing. It's essential to educate them about the strict anti-doping rules and regulations.

Our Commitment

NADA India is committed to providing comprehensive anti-doping education to RTP athletes. We aim to ensure they understand their rights, responsibilities, and the consequences of doping.

Education Programs

  • Specialized workshops and seminars
  • Regular awareness campaigns
  • Online learning modules
  • One-on-one counseling sessions
